Creating something is always about more than one product, one person, or one impact. Even the smallest projects can be used by tens of thousands, and dramatically impact day-to-day lives. Turning beautiful iOS apps and tweaks into reality, then, means more than one idea, one person, or one goal need to be at play. Rinse is a Developer Collective, made up of small, casual teams of developers who are personal friends and have decided to take projects and clean, prepare, and maintain them for the future.
